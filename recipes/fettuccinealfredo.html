Fettuccine Alfredo

Description

Fettuccine Alfredo, a classic Italian-American indulgence, is a pasta dish that exudes creamy elegance with every bite. This luxurious creation features broad ribbons of fettuccine pasta, perfectly cooked to a tender al dente, and dressed in a velvety Alfredo sauce that's a symphony of butter, heavy cream, and freshly grated Parmesan cheese. The result is a rich, luscious sauce that clings to each strand of pasta, enveloping it in a decadent embrace. The simplicity of the ingredients allows the flavors to shine, making Fettuccine Alfredo a timeless favorite that's both comforting and sophisticated. Topped with a sprinkle of parsley for a burst of freshness, this dish is the epitome of Italian-American comfort food, offering a taste of pure indulgence in every forkful.

Ingredients

  • Fettuccine pasta - 8 ounces
  • Unsalted butter - 1/2 cup
  • Heavy cream - 1 cup
  • Garlic cloves, minced - 2 cloves
  • Grated Parmesan cheese - 1 1/2 cups
  • Salt - 1/2 teaspoon (or to taste)
  • Black pepper - 1/4 teaspoon (or to taste)
  • Nutmeg - 1/4 teaspoon (optional, for flavor)
  • Fresh parsley, chopped, for garnish

Instructions

  1. Boil a large pot of salted water.
  2. Add 8 ounces of fettuccine pasta to the boiling water.
  3. Cook the pasta until it's al dente, following the package instructions.
  4. While the pasta cooks, prepare the Alfredo sauce:
    1. Melt 1/2 cup of unsalted butter in a saucepan over low heat.
    2. Stir in 1 cup of heavy cream and let it simmer gently.
    3. Add 2 cloves of minced garlic and simmer for another 2 minutes.
    4. Stir in 1 1/2 cups of grated Parmesan cheese until the sauce is smooth and creamy.
    5. Season the sauce with 1/2 teaspoon of salt and 1/4 teaspoon of black pepper (adjust to taste).
    6. Add 1/4 teaspoon of nutmeg for extra flavor (optional).
    7. Remove the sauce from heat.
  5. Drain the cooked fettuccine pasta and return it to the pot.
  6. Pour the Alfredo sauce over the pasta and toss to coat the noodles evenly.
  7. Transfer the creamy Fettuccine Alfredo to serving plates or a platter.
  8. Garnish with chopped fresh parsley for a pop of color and flavor.
  9. Serve hot and enjoy your homemade Fettuccine Alfredo!
