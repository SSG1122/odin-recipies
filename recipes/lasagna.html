Lasagna

Description

Lasagna, often referred to as the ultimate comfort food, is a glorious Italian masterpiece that tantalizes taste buds with layers of culinary delight. This dish is a symphony of flavors and textures, featuring wide sheets of pasta lovingly layered with rich, meaty Bolognese sauce, creamy béchamel, and a generous sprinkling of grated Parmesan and mozzarella cheese. The result is a harmonious marriage of tender pasta, savory sauce, and velvety cheese that's baked to golden perfection. With each forkful, you'll experience the delightful contrast of crispy, cheesy edges and the luscious, melt-in-your-mouth interior. Lasagna is a timeless classic that brings families and friends together at the dinner table, and its irresistible aroma wafting from the oven is enough to make anyone's mouth water.

Ingredients

  • Lasagna noodles - 12 sheets
  • Ground beef or Italian sausage - 1 pound
  • Onion, finely chopped - 1 medium
  • Garlic cloves, minced - 3 cloves
  • Canned crushed tomatoes - 28 ounces
  • Tomato paste - 2 tablespoons
  • Olive oil - 2 tablespoons
  • Dried basil - 1 teaspoon
  • Dried oregano - 1 teaspoon
  • Salt - 1 teaspoon (or to taste)
  • Black pepper - 1/2 teaspoon (or to taste)
  • Ricotta cheese - 15 ounces
  • Egg - 1 large
  • Grated Parmesan cheese - 1/2 cup
  • Shredded mozzarella cheese - 2 cups
  • Fresh basil leaves, for garnish

Instructions

  1. Boil a large pot of water and add a pinch of salt.
  2. Cook 12 lasagna noodles until they are al dente, following package instructions.
  3. Drain the noodles and rinse them with cold water to prevent sticking; set aside.
  4. Prepare the Bolognese sauce:
    1. In a large skillet, heat 2 tablespoons of olive oil over medium heat.
    2. Add 1 medium chopped onion and sauté until translucent (about 3-5 minutes).
    3. Stir in 3 cloves of minced garlic and cook for an additional 1-2 minutes.
    4. Add 1 pound of ground beef (or a mix of beef and pork) and cook until browned.
    5. Combine 28 ounces of canned crushed tomatoes, 2 tablespoons of tomato paste, 1 teaspoon of dried basil, 1 teaspoon of dried oregano, 1 teaspoon of salt, and 1/2 teaspoon of black pepper into the skillet.
    6. Simmer the sauce for 20-25 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  5. In a bowl, mix 15 ounces of ricotta cheese, 1 large egg, and 1/2 cup of grated Parmesan cheese until smooth; set aside.
  6. Assemble the lasagna:
    1. Spread a thin layer of Bolognese sauce in a 9x13-inch baking dish.
    2. Place a layer of cooked lasagna noodles on top.
    3. Spread half of the ricotta cheese mixture evenly over the noodles.
    4. Add a layer of shredded mozzarella cheese.
    5. Repeat the layers: Bolognese sauce, noodles, ricotta mixture, and mozzarella cheese.
    6. Finish with a final layer of noodles and top with the remaining Bolognese sauce and mozzarella cheese.
  7. Cover the baking dish with aluminum foil and bake at 375°F (190°C) for 25-30 minutes.
  8. Remove the foil and bake for an additional 10-15 minutes, or until the cheese is golden and bubbly.
  9. Let it cool for a few minutes, garnish with fresh basil leaves, slice, and serve your homemade lasagna!
